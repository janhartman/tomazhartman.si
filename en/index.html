Mosaics, sculptures, land art

Apsyrtides

Mosaics, sculptures and land art are just the memories of our paths, the sea, mysterious virgin forests, the discovery of ancient stories and lost trails in magical nature or in landscapes shaped by man for centuries, but almost completely abandoned today. The search for mosaic pieces, prehistoric shards, pebbles and driftwood intertwines mosaics and sculptures with antiquity and paths, which are not just adventures, treasure hunts, but become pilgrimages and searches for something inexplicable and beautiful.
